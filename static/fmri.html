How fMRI Analysis Works
📡
BOLD Signal Processing
Our model processes Blood-Oxygen-Level-Dependent (BOLD) signals that reflect neural activity across brain regions.
🤖
Deep Learning Classifier
A 3D convolutional neural network trained on functional connectivity matrices identifies schizophrenia patterns.
🗺️
Spatial Feature Extraction
Spatial biomarkers are extracted from prefrontal, temporal, and parietal regions known to differ in schizophrenia.
📊
Confidence Scoring
Each prediction is accompanied by a confidence score to help researchers gauge the certainty of results.
